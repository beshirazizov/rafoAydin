RAFO - Azerbaijani DJ | Sound Producer | Remixer

RAFO - is a music producer who cooperates with famous labels such as Road Story Records (U.S.A California), 48records (Turkish), Deep Strips (Ecuador), Maniana Records (Greece), Yeiskomp Records (USA), Media Blackout (Los Angeles), Disguise Records (Greece), Disco Balls Records (Miami) and Cafe De Anatolia.

His music is played on radio shows in Europe, USA, Brazil, and Turkey. His singles and remixes are played on famous Russian and Ukrainian radio channels such as DFM-Moscow, Radio Record, Megapolis FM, Radio Europa Plus, Soundpark, Radio NRJ, where he won "NRJ Music Awards 2016"
